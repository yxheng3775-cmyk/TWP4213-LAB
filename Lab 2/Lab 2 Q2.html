Sports in Malaysia

Malaysia is home to a variety of sports enjoyed by people of all ages. Popular sports include badminton, football, and hockey.

Popular Sports

  • Badminton

    Malaysia has produced world-class players like Lee Chong Wei.

  • Football

    Played widely at both amateur and professional levels.

  • Hockey

    Malaysia competes internationally in field hockey tournaments.
